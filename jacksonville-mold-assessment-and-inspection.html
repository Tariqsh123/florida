Our Step-By-Step Mold Inspection Process

Every Jacksonville property has unique mold challenges. Our structured process ensures safe removal, contamination control, and lasting protection, customized to the city’s subtropical climate, high humidity, and hurricane-related risks.

Step #1 Thorough Home Assessment and Moisture Detection Our team inspects every area, including hidden spaces like attics, crawlspaces, walls, and HVAC systems. We use moisture meters, thermal imaging, and air sampling to uncover damp zones invisible to the naked eye. Jacksonville homes in Riverside, San Marco, and Mandarin often experience extra moisture from coastal proximity, river flooding, and heavy afternoon storms. Identifying the root source of mold early allows us to create a precise remediation plan. This step ensures complete removal, protects indoor air quality, and safeguards your home’s structural integrity while preventing future mold growth in Florida’s subtropical climate.

Step #2 Controlled Containment of Affected Areas Before any removal work begins, we isolate contaminated zones using physical barriers and negative air pressure. HVAC systems, furniture, and personal items are carefully protected. Jacksonville’s humid climate and frequent tropical storms accelerate spore spread, making containment crucial. Sealing the affected areas prevents cross-contamination and keeps the rest of your home safe. This method also maintains indoor air quality while allowing our team to work efficiently. Proper containment reduces the risk of recurring mold problems and ensures the remediation process is completed safely, providing homeowners confidence in the integrity and cleanliness of their property.

Step #3 Safe Mold Removal and Treatment Application We carefully remove materials that cannot be salvaged, including damaged drywall, insulation, and wood. Surfaces that can be saved are treated with EPA-approved antimicrobials to eliminate residual spores and prevent regrowth. Jacksonville’s high humidity and coastal conditions require thorough treatment to ensure mold does not return. Our certified team follows strict safety protocols to protect residents, workers, and property. Combining removal with antimicrobial treatment restores a safe living environment, preserves structural integrity, and prepares the home for repairs or long-term moisture control measures. This step ensures lasting protection and a healthier home for Florida homeowners.

Step #4 Moisture Control and Prevention Measures Mold will return if underlying moisture problems persist. We identify leaks, ventilation gaps, drainage issues, and areas with consistently high humidity. Homes near the St. Johns River, coastal zones, and low-lying neighborhoods are particularly at risk. We provide recommendations for structural repairs, improved airflow, and dehumidification solutions to prevent water accumulation. By addressing these issues, we reduce the chance of mold recurrence, improve indoor air quality, and protect the property’s long-term integrity. Our preventative strategies give Jacksonville homeowners peace of mind, keeping homes safe, dry, and mold-free even during the hurricane season and Florida’s humid summers.
